Work With Me

I help leadership teams make better technology decisions across strategy, governance, diligence, AI adoption, cybersecurity, and engineering execution.

I work with founders, CEOs, boards, investors, and technology leaders when technology decisions carry business risk. My work is usually focused on aligning technology, people, process, architecture, and governance with business outcomes.
