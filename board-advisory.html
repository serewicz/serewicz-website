Open Source

Platform Modernization

Guide cloud-native, Kubernetes, observability, FinOps, policy-as-code, and compliance evidence decisions.

Governance Focus

The goal is not to turn the board into a technical review committee. The goal is to make material technology decisions visible, owned, and connected to strategy.

  • What risks require board visibility?
  • Which decisions need executive ownership?
  • Where does technology constrain growth?
  • How should AI adoption be governed?
  • What evidence supports management confidence?
